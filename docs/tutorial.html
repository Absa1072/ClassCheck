Welcome to ClassCheck

ClassCheck makes tracking your classes and attendance simple, secure, and stress-free!

How it Works

  • Students log in using their Rutgers email
  • Choose the class you're attending
  • Mark your attendance in just one click
  • Professors view attendance automatically

For Students

Log in → View your classes → Mark attendance → You're done!

For Professors

Log in → Manage class lists → View students’ attendance records easily.

Secure Login

All accounts are created securely through Rutgers email authentication powered by Auth0.
