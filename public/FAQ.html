ClassCheck makes tracking your classes and attendance simple, secure, and stress-free!

Some commonly asked questions about our site.

  • How does the website track my attendance?

    • The website will ask for your browsers location and then compare your location to the location of your desired class.

  • How does my professor see my attendance?

    • The professors can see your attendance as it is marked in their system instantly.

  • How can I mark my attendance for each class?

    • Once you login, click on the class for which you need to mark your attendance, after that, the website will mark your attendance and store it in the system.
